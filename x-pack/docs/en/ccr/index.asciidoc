[[cross-cluster-replication]]
= Replicating indices across clusters

:ccr:             cross cluster replication
:CCR:             Cross cluster replication
[partintro]
--
{CCR} (CCR) synchronizes indices in two or more clusters using active-passive
replication. Replicating indices across clusters can increase resiliency,
facilitate disaster recovery and data migration, and enable queries to be
processed in closer geographic proximity to your users.
