[[troubleshooting]]
= Troubleshooting


This section provides a series of troubleshooting guides aimed at helping users
fix problems an Elasticsearch deployment might encounter.

Reporting and diagnosing the problems presented in this section is assisted
by the <<health-api,health API>>.

If none of the following guides are related to your issue, you can still get help:

== Getting help

- For users with active subscription, you get access to support via:

* Go directly to the Support Portal: http://support.elastic.co

* From the {n} Console: Go to the 
 link:https://cloud.elastic.co/support{ess-baymax}[Support page] or select the
 support icon, that looks like a life preserver, on any page in the console.

* Contact us by email: support@elastic.co

- For users without active subscription, hop on our 
  https://discuss.elastic.co/[Elastic community forums] and get answers from
the experts in the community, including people from Elastic.
